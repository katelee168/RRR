What we do

  • Read


    Who doesn't love reading? We collect book donations and distribute them to low-income schools.

  • Record


    But we take it one step further. At each of our events, volunteers read and record the books, storing them in our online database.

  • Replay


    Voila! Now students and teachers can easily access e-books in our online database, connecting communities through the power of storytelling.

  • Our
    Dream

  • In 2012,
    21% of American adults
    read below a fifth grade level.

    We aim to decrease illiteracy by providing children with books and audio recordings, instilling a love for reading.
