Recorder: Detailed Instructions

(Note: Chrome, Safari, or Firefox are recommended.)
1. Be sure to test your audio and speak loudly, clearly, and with animation!
2. Click the red record button to begin and pause the recording.
3. Clap to signal a page turn.
4. When finished, press the small stop button to stop recording (Note: Stopping and not submitting will erase the previous recording. Do not stop until the very end.)
5. An audio player will appear underneath the record button. Click play to playback your recording. If you are not satisfied with it, record again!
6. Finally, submit your recording! (Scroll down for the submit button.)
